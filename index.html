Hey! Thanks for trying the game. Here's some help to get you situationed:
Winning
You win the game if you get to 20. It's harder than it sounds!
Alternatively, you win if you have a card in your tableau with a Do: that says 'win the game' and you fulfill its When: condition.
Playing The Game
First, click the main button with the icon on it. This will give you a that allows you to buy cards in the market.
Every card has a When: condition that will activate when its in your tableau. When it's activated, it will then do whatever its Do: is.
The tricky thing is you don't have much resources. You lose a each time you click the button to get more to buy more cards.
And you only have so many cards that can fit in your market. () Same thing with your tableau! ()
But the cards you buy give you more of these resources! But only if you fulfill its When: condition.
Some of the cards do weird stuff!
